
*******************************************Niloofar_Jason_Bogdan Final Project*******************************************

In this game your goal is to pass the obstacles, meet and kill the enemy, so the door will be open and you get the potion.
There are 2 green spheres that boost your health.

Don't forget:

- you can shoot with : Right Click
- Reload : R
- Crouch : Shift
- Prone: Hold shift
- Shrink: E
- Jump: Space
- Tab: inventory
